MTN stands for : Mobile Telepphone Network
MTN Nigeria was founded in 2001, The first call on its network was made on May 16, 2001, this is the first and oldest mobile network in Nigeria. MTN Nigeria is a member of the MTN group , with over 66 million subscribers of which 38 million are internet subscribers. They are seen as the GSM market Leader.
